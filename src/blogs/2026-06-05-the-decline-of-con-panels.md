I'm writing this while sitting in the ending minutes of a panel at our local comic con, it's the audience Q and A portion and is mostly people "asking questions" to hear themselves speak. This is normal and not what I think marks the decline of the panel as both an act of community building and one of socialization.

I have some opinions about these fandom events and the over commercialization of everything.
